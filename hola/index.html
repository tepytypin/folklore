about folklore

genres explored

Departing from the upbeat pop production of its predecessors, Folklore consists of mellow ballads driven by neo-classical instruments, pursuing indie folk, alternative rock, and electroacoustic styles. Aaron Dessner from The National and Jack Antonoff from Bleachers are the main producers, both adding their own signature sound for a well rounded album with both highs and lows of reflective emotion.

lyrics explored

Swift dabbles into storytelling, literally creating her own 'folklore'. Each song has its own story to tell in its own voice, ranging from her own house's history to an ode to romanticism.
